Operational Scenario

Sentinel simulates an analyst workflow where public online indicators are reviewed for signs of hybrid threat activity. The system highlights coordinated narrative amplification, unusual engagement spikes, bot-like account behavior, and suspicious URL sharing. The objective is not attribution, but early triage and situational awareness.

Methodology & Risk Logic

Risk Score Formula:

risk = bot score + engagement anomaly + sentiment weight + suspicious keyword score + URL indicator + coordination flag

The current implementation uses rule-based AI simulation for transparent and explainable scoring. In a production version, these components could be replaced by NLP models, anomaly detection algorithms, and live OSINT feeds.

Ethics & Limitations

Sentinel is a proof-of-concept analytical prototype. It uses synthetic intelligence scenarios and does not access private accounts, classified sources, or live platform APIs. The system does not perform attribution or surveillance. It is designed to support analyst review, not replace human judgment.
